2.What is Polymorphism? explain types?
	Poly stands for many
	Morphism stands for form
	Polymorphism is multiple forms of implementation.
	It is of two types
a) Method overloading/ Static Binding /Compiletime Polymorphism
	class name and method name are same . Arguments are different.
b) Method overriding / Dynamic Binding /Runtime Polymorphism
	classes are different but methods name are same.
